# Mobile App: Now Available for Android

We're thrilled to announce that Habuks is now available on Android devices! After months of development and beta testing, our mobile app is ready to help you manage your group finances on the go.

## Why Mobile Matters

Our data shows that **78% of our users** access Habuks from mobile devices. Until now, they've been using our responsive web app, but a native Android experience brings significant improvements.

## What's New in the App

### Instant Notifications
Never miss important updates:
- Contribution reminders
- Payment confirmations
- Group announcements
- Meeting schedules

### Offline Access
Check your records even without internet:
- View contribution history
- Access member contacts
- Review group rules
- See upcoming events

### Faster Performance
The native app is optimized for speed:
- Quick loading times
- Smooth animations
- Responsive interface
- Low data usage

## Key Features

Here's what you can do with the Habuks Android app:

1. **Dashboard** - See your group's financial summary at a glance
2. **Contributions** - Track payments and view history
3. **Members** - Manage member information and contact details
4. **Reports** - Generate and share financial reports
5. **Settings** - Customize notifications and preferences

## How to Download

Getting the app is easy:

1. Open the Google Play Store on your Android device
2. Search for "Habuks"
3. Tap "Install"
4. Sign in with your existing account

> Already have a Habuks account? Your data automatically syncs to the mobile app!

## System Requirements

- Android 8.0 (Oreo) or higher
- 50 MB of storage space
- Active internet for initial sync
