
# Getting Started with Habuks

Welcome to Habuks. This guide walks you through the essential steps to set up your organization and begin managing your records confidently.

---

## Overview

In this article, you will learn how to:

* Create your account
* Set up your organization
* Assign roles and permissions
* Add members
* Record your first transaction
* Create projects
* Generate reports
* Follow a simple monthly routine

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account

After subscribing, you will receive a login email.

**To get started:**

1. Go to [www.habuks.com](http://www.habuks.com)
2. Log in using your email
3. Verify your email address
4. Create a strong password

**Recommended:** Enable two-factor authentication for additional security.

---

## Step 2: Set Up Your Organization

When you log in for the first time, you’ll be asked to enter your organization details.

You will need to provide:

* Organization name
* Organization type (e.g., community group, NGO, association)
* Main currency
* Financial year start date

These settings affect your financial reports. Make sure the information is correct before saving.

---

## Step 3: Configure Roles and Permissions

Before recording financial data, define who has access.

Common roles include:

* **Administrator** – full system access
* **Treasurer** – manages financial records
* **Member** – limited or view-only access

**Best practice:** Avoid giving full financial control to a single person without oversight.

---

## Step 4: Add Members

To add members:

1. Go to **Members**
2. Click **Add Member**
3. Enter their details
4. Assign a role

Only assign editing permissions to trusted individuals.

---

## Step 5: Record Your First Transaction

To add a financial record:

1. Go to **Finance**
2. Click **New Record**
3. Select the type:

   * Contribution (income)
   * Expense
   * Project-related transaction
4. Enter:

   * Date
   * Amount
   * Category
   * Description
   * Upload receipt (optional but recommended)
5. Review and save

The system records the information exactly as entered. Always double-check before saving.

---

## Step 6: Create a Project (Optional)

If your organization runs initiatives or programs:

1. Go to **Projects**
2. Click **Create Project**
3. Set the project name and budget
4. Link related transactions

Projects help you track income and expenses separately.

---

## Step 7: Generate Reports

To view financial summaries:

1. Go to **Reports**
2. Select the report type:

   * Contribution summary
   * Expense breakdown
   * Project balance
   * Member contribution history

Review reports carefully before sharing them with members or external parties.

---

## Recommended Monthly Routine

To maintain accurate records:

* Review all transactions
* Compare totals with your cash or bank records
* Download a backup or export
* Share a financial summary with your leadership team

Consistent monthly review prevents confusion and disputes.
