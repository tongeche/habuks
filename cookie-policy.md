
# Cookie Policy

Last update: 04 August 2025

This section provides detailed information about the specific cookies used on the Habuks platform.

Cookies are categorized based on their purpose. Some cookies are essential for the Service to function, while others help us understand usage patterns and improve performance.

Where required by applicable law, non-essential cookies (such as analytics cookies) are only set after you provide consent.

The table below outlines:

- The name of each cookie
- The provider responsible for the cookie
- Its purpose
- How long it remains on your device

Strictly necessary cookies are required for core platform functionality and cannot be disabled through our systems.

Analytics cookies help us understand how the platform is used so we can improve reliability and usability. These cookies do not allow us to directly identify individual users.

If you would like to manage your cookie preferences, you may do so through your browser settings or the cookie consent tool provided on our website (where available).

| Cookie Name           | Provider         | Type               | Purpose                                                        | Duration                              |
| --------------------- | ---------------- | ------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| `habuks_session`      | Habuks           | Strictly Necessary | Maintains secure user session after login                      | Session (deleted when browser closes) |
| `habuks_auth`         | Habuks           | Strictly Necessary | Authenticates logged-in users and prevents unauthorized access | 7 days                                |
| `csrf_token`          | Habuks           | Strictly Necessary | Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks            | Session                               |
| `_ga`                 | Google Analytics | Analytics          | Distinguishes users for traffic analysis                       | 2 years                               |
| `_ga_[container-id]`  | Google Analytics | Analytics          | Maintains session state for analytics                          | 2 years                               |
| `_gid`                | Google Analytics | Analytics          | Tracks user behavior within a single day                       | 24 hours                              |
| `_gat`                | Google Analytics | Analytics          | Throttles request rate                                         | 1 minute                              |
| `supabase-auth-token` | Supabase         | Strictly Necessary | Manages authentication state                                   | 1 week                                |
| `sb-refresh-token`    | Supabase         | Strictly Necessary | Refreshes authentication session                               | 1 week                                |
| `cookie_consent`      | Habuks           | Functional         | Stores user cookie preferences                                 | 6 months                              |
| `locale`              | Habuks           | Functional         | Remembers language selection                                   | 6 months                              |
