JONGOL Brand Colors (derived from logo.png)

Primary
- JONGOL Green: #398426 (57, 132, 38) - primary brand color for headers, buttons, links, key icons.

Secondary
- Leaf Green: #6CA71F (108, 167, 31) - accents, hover states, highlights.
- Forest Green: #216317 (33, 99, 23) - dark tone for text, outlines, footer.

Support / Neutrals
- Light Leaf: #B1CBA6 (177, 203, 166) - soft backgrounds, section panels, subtle borders.
- Off White: #FEFEFE (254, 254, 254) - page background, negative space.

Usage notes
- Use JONGOL Green + Off White as the default pairing.
- Use Forest Green for body text to maintain contrast on light backgrounds.
- Limit Leaf Green accents to small UI moments (badges, highlights, hover).
